About InvoxiaGST

Welcome to InvoxiaGST, the most refined platform for entrepreneurs, creators, and small businesses looking to launch their online presence with ease and sophistication.

  • Tailored Multi-Store Architecture: Each merchant gets a personalized store URL with complete data isolation.
  • Insights-Driven Growth: Real-time analytics dashboard to track sales, revenue, and customer behavior.
  • Seamless Payments & Invoicing: GST-compliant automated invoices and secure payment handling.
  • All-in-One Business Management: Manage products, inventory, orders, and customers in one platform.
  • Built for Scale & Trust: Secure infrastructure designed to grow with your business.
  • Demo-Ready & Safe: Sandbox mode with simulated payments for risk-free testing.

Our Company

Invoxia Technologies Ltd. is at the forefront of e-commerce innovation, headquartered in Bengaluru. We are driven by the ethos of simplicity, reliability, and premium user experience.

With deep expertise in cloud deployment, analytics, and scalable architecture, we empower merchants to create elegant, powerful online stores without complexity.

Our mission is simple: to deliver a platform that feels premium in design, yet effortless to use.
